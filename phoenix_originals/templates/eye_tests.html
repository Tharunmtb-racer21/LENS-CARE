Understanding Color Vision Deficiency

Deuteranopia (Red-Green)

Most common type. Difficulty distinguishing between red and green hues. Caused by absence of medium-wavelength cones.

Protanopia (Red)

Reduced sensitivity to red light. Red appears darker and less vibrant. Caused by absence of long-wavelength cones.

Tritanopia (Blue-Yellow)

Rare condition. Difficulty distinguishing between blue and yellow. Caused by absence of short-wavelength cones.
